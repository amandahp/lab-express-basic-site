Time

For sure Time is one of my favorite Pink Floyd songs,
but I could say this about countless other songs.
This is a page dedicated to a band that not only sings songs,
but writes lyrics that impact lives.
I get chills every time I hear Hey You,
the melody of Pigs or On The Turning Away,
in fact, all Pink Floyd songs give me some feeling,
but only Time reminds me that I'm getting old,
that's why she's so special .
